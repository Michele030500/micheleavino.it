DASHN is a Saudi Lifestyle brand that aims to digitally transform the Made -to-Measure process of traditional Thobes. Proud of their Arabian Heritage, but frustrated at the time -consuming, and often disappointing trips to the tailor, the Founders created DASHN with the aim of offering a more modern approach that celebrates Saudi culture as well as functionality.
